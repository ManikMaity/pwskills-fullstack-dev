Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites for the internet or a private network. Web developers, or "devs", use a variety of coding languages to create and maintain websites. They are responsible for the technical aspects of a website, such as its performance and capacity. They may also create content for the site.

There are three main types of web development:

  • Front-end development
  • Back-end development
  • Full stack development
